Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1964, Bharati Vidyapeeth has evolved from a modest educational initiative to a prestigious deemed university recognized nationwide. The Department of Management Studies, established in 1984, stands as a beacon of business education excellence with a vision to nurture future-ready corporate leaders.

Our journey spans decades of academic innovation and industry collaboration. The department has consistently upheld rigorous academic standards, earning accolades for educational quality and research contributions. Our alumni network extends across leading organizations globally, with graduates making significant impacts in their respective fields.

The beautiful campus you see today reflects our growth and commitment to providing state-of-the-art facilities that support a holistic learning environment.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become successful business leaders and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in management education, fostering innovation, research, and sustainable development.
