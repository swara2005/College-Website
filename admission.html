Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for Admission

MBA Program

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Valid CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT score with minimum percentile as per university norms
  • Minimum 2 years of work experience preferred
  • Group Discussion & Personal Interview performance
  • Final year students can apply with provisional degree
  • Valid score in university entrance test (if applicable)

BBA Program

  • 10+2 with minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST candidates)
  • English proficiency with minimum 60% marks
  • Valid score in university entrance test
  • Personal Interview performance
  • Age limit: Maximum 22 years as on 1st July of admission year
  • Basic computer knowledge preferred

BCA Program

  • 10+2 with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2 with minimum 50% marks
  • Valid score in university entrance test
  • Basic computer knowledge and programming aptitude
  • Age limit: Maximum 22 years as on 1st July of admission year
  • Personal Interview performance

MCA Program

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Bachelor's in CS/IT/Mathematics/Statistics preferred
  • Valid score in university entrance test
  • Mathematics as a subject in graduation
  • Personal Interview performance
  • Final year students can apply with provisional degree

Admission Process

Steps to Join Our Institution

1. Online Registration

Complete online registration with personal and academic details

  • Fill application form with accurate information
  • Upload required documents (marksheets, certificates)
  • Pay application fee online
  • Submit entrance exam scores (if applicable)

2. Entrance Examination

Appear for university entrance examination

  • Download admit card from university portal
  • Appear for entrance test at designated center
  • Test includes aptitude and subject knowledge
  • Results announced within 2 weeks

3. Selection Process

Complete selection process including interviews

  • Group Discussion (for MBA)
  • Personal Interview
  • Document verification
  • Final merit list preparation

4. Admission Confirmation

Complete admission formalities

  • Pay admission fees
  • Submit original documents
  • Complete medical examination
  • Orientation program attendance

Important Notes:

  • All dates and deadlines will be announced on the university website
  • Keep checking your registered email for updates
  • Original documents must be submitted during admission
  • Seats are limited and admission is subject to availability
